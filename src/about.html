About MEIJIA DENTAL

Hong Kong originated, Shenzhen Bao'an based, built for reliable dental manufacturing.

MEIJIA DENTAL serves clinics, distributors and dental laboratories with disciplined restoration production, practical communication and steady case support.

Company Introduction

A focused dental laboratory for fixed, implant, removable and orthodontic work.

MEIJIA DENTAL originated in Hong Kong and developed its production capability in Shenzhen. Since establishing Shenzhen production in 2005, the company has focused on dependable dental restorations for everyday clinical needs and partner laboratory workflows.

The Bao'an factory is organized around digital case intake, CAD design, material processing, technician finishing, quality inspection and dispatch. Each case is reviewed for fit, morphology, contact, occlusion, shade and surface finish before delivery.
